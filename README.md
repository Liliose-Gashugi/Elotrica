# Elotrica — Rwanda's Luxury EV Transportation

Elotrica is Rwanda's first fully electric VIP transportation service, offering premium travel with zero direct emissions. This is the official website for Elotrica, showcasing the service, fleet, and enabling customers to book rides via WhatsApp.

## About Elotrica

Elotrica provides professional electric vehicle transportation for:

- **Corporate clients** — executive travel, team transport, and priority dispatch
- **Individuals** — airport transfers, special occasions, and hourly charter
- **Visitors & tourists** — city tours, gorilla trekking transfers, and multi-day itineraries

All vehicles are late-model EVs, fully charged before every ride, and driven by vetted professional drivers.
